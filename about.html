About GraceChurch

Discover our story, mission, and the faith that unites us as a community dedicated to serving Christ.

Our Story

Founded in 1985, GraceChurch began as a small gathering of believers in a local community center. What started as a humble beginning has grown into a vibrant community of faith, hope, and love.

Through decades of faithful service, we've witnessed God's transformative power in countless lives. Our journey has been marked by both challenges and triumphs, each strengthening our commitment to spreading the Gospel and serving our community.

Today, we continue to build on the foundation laid by our founders, embracing new opportunities to share God's love while staying true to the biblical principles that have guided us from the beginning.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God passionately, serve others selflessly, and share the Gospel courageously in our community and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a thriving, multi-generational church that transforms lives through authentic worship, biblical teaching, and compassionate outreach.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the final authority for faith and practice.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Salvation

We believe in salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, who died for our sins and rose again.

Our Journey Through Time

1985 - Humble Beginnings

Founded by Pastor John and Mary Smith

Started with 12 members meeting in a community center, focused on neighborhood outreach and Bible study.

1992 - First Building

Purchased our first church building

Acquired a small building that allowed us to expand our ministries and serve more people in the community.

2005 - Growth & Expansion

Built current sanctuary and facilities

Constructed our current worship center and added educational wings to accommodate growing membership.

2018 - Digital Ministry Launch

Started online streaming and outreach

Embracing technology to reach people globally through online services and digital discipleship programs.

2023 - Present Day

Serving 1500+ members weekly

Continuing to grow as a multi-generational church with diverse ministries serving all age groups and needs.
