About Our Church

Discover our story, mission, and the heart behind RCCG City Of Joy Parish - Rivers Province 10 Headquarters.

Our Story

A Community of Faith, Hope & Love

RCCG City Of Joy is the Rivers Province 10 Headquarters of the Redeemed Christian Church of God. We are a vibrant, spirit-filled community dedicated to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ and making disciples of all nations.

Founded on the solid rock of God's word, our church has grown to become a beacon of hope in our community. We believe in the transformative power of God's love and are committed to nurturing spiritual growth, fostering genuine fellowship, and serving our community with compassion.

As part of the worldwide RCCG family under the leadership of Pastor E.A. Adeboye (General Overseer), we are committed to the mission of planting churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries, and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Guided by God's word, we are committed to fulfilling our divine mandate.

Our Vision

To make heaven, to take as many people with us, to have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations, to accomplish the above, holiness will be our lifestyle, to plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries, and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.

Our Mission

To spread the gospel of Jesus Christ to the ends of the earth, raise leaders who will transform their communities, nurture believers into mature Christians through sound biblical teaching, create an atmosphere of worship where God's presence is experienced, and extend God's love to our community through compassionate service and outreach.

What We Believe

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do as a church family.

Biblical Authority

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God - our ultimate guide for faith and life.

Love & Unity

We are called to love God and love one another, building a community of genuine fellowship and care.

Holiness

We pursue a lifestyle of holiness, being set apart for God's purposes in every area of our lives.

Spirit-Led Worship

We embrace Spirit-filled worship that ushers us into God's presence and transforms our hearts.

Evangelism

We are passionate about sharing the gospel and reaching the lost with the message of salvation.

Discipleship

We are committed to making disciples who will go on to make other disciples for Christ.

Service

We believe in serving God and others with humility, using our gifts for the advancement of His kingdom.

Prayer

We depend on prayer as our lifeline, believing that prayer moves the hand of God in every situation.
