Purpose

Why We Give

Giving is an act of worship and obedience that reflects our trust in God's provision.

Biblical Principle

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." - 2 Corinthians 9:7

Community Impact

Your contributions directly support outreach programs, community assistance, and spiritual growth initiatives that transform lives in our neighborhood and beyond.

Sustainable Ministry

Regular giving ensures the continuity of our ministries, church operations, and ability to respond to community needs both locally and globally.
