Regular Weekly Schedule

Join us throughout the week for worship, teaching, and fellowship

Sunday Workers Meeting

7:00 AM - 8:00 AM

Leadership preparation and prayer for all church workers and volunteers before services begin.

Sunday School

8:00 AM - 9:00 AM

Age-appropriate Bible study classes for everyone from children to adults.

Sunday Main Service

9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Worship service with prayer and an inspiring message from Pastor Solomon Marega.

Tuesday Digging Deep

6:00 PM - 7:15 PM

In-depth Bible study session exploring Scripture in greater detail.

Thursday Faith Clinic

6:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Teaching and prayer session focused on building and strengthening faith.
