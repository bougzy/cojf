About Our Church

Welcome to City Of Joy Parish

We are the Redeemed Christian Church of God, Rivers Province 10 Headquarters. Our mission is to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ, raise godly leaders, and transform communities through the power of God's love.

Under the leadership of Pastor Solomon Marega (PIC Rivers Province 10), we are committed to nurturing spiritual growth, fostering genuine fellowship, and making disciples of all nations.
